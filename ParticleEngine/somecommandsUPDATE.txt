esc to quit
r resets
R resets to 5 particles
q add 3 particles
1 add 1 particle
Q remove 5 particles

t Speed Increase
T Speed decrease
w y direction up
s y direction down
a x direction left
s x direction right
e z direction far
f z direction close

x toggles camera rotation around x axis
y toggles camera rotation around y axis
z toggles camera rotation around z axis

h hurricane mode (extra)
c hit multiple times to toggle the spray mode (tight and lose)
b enable/disable backface culling
